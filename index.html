FAQ

Welcome to our FAQ section, where we aim to provide you with clear and comprehensive answers to common questions about our services. Whether you're curious about the benefits of different massage techniques, or you have specific inquiries about our offerings, you'll find the information you need here. We understand the importance of making informed decisions about your health and well-being, so we've compiled detailed responses to help guide you.

Swedish Massage is a gentle type of full-body massage that's ideal for people who:
  • are new to massage
  • want to release tension
  • desire gentle touch
It can help to relax the entire body, increase the level of oxygen in the blood, improve circulation and flexibility, and ease tension.

Sports Massage is designed to help athletes of every kind, from world-class professionals to weekend joggers. The particulars of the sports massage technique are specific to the athlete's sport of choice. Focus is given to areas of the body that are overused and stressed from repetitive and often aggressive movements.

Myofascial Release Massage involves applying gentle sustained pressure into the myofascial connective tissue restrictions to eliminate pain and restore motion. This type of therapy is used to treat skeletal muscle immobility and pain by relaxing contracted muscles, improving blood and lymphatic circulation, and stimulating the stretch reflex in muscles.

Prenatal/Postnatal Massage can be highly beneficial for both the mother and the baby. It helps to reduce stress, decrease swelling, relieve aches and pains, and reduce anxiety and depression. It's important to receive this type of massage from a trained and certified prenatal massage therapist to ensure both the mother's and the baby's safety.
